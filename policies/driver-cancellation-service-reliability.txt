
RAJLO Driver Cancellation & Service Reliability Policy
Effective Date: May 17, 2026
This Driver Cancellation & Service Reliability Policy governs the use of the RAJLO driver platform by independent drivers operating through the RajLo transportation marketplace in Jamaica. By accessing or using the RAJLO Driver App, drivers agree to comply with this policy and all applicable Jamaican laws and regulations.

1. Independent Driver Status
1.1 Independent Contractor Relationship
Drivers using the RAJLO platform are independent transportation providers and are not employees, agents, partners, or representatives of RAJLO.
RAJLO operates solely as a technology platform that connects riders with independent drivers. Drivers are fully responsible for:
Their own conduct and behavior
Vehicle condition and maintenance
Driving decisions
Route selection
Passenger interactions
Compliance with Jamaican traffic laws and Transport Authority regulations
Insurance, licensing, permits, taxes, and operating expenses
1.2 No Transportation Liability
RAJLO does not own, operate, manage, or control vehicles and is not responsible for:
Driver actions or omissions
Passenger conduct
Traffic incidents or accidents
Injuries, damages, theft, or losses
Delays, route issues, or missed pickups
Disputes between drivers and passengers
Service interruptions caused by network, GPS, mobile carrier, weather, or technical failures
All transportation services are provided directly by independent drivers.

2. Driver Cancellation Policy
2.1 Acceptance of Ride Requests
Once a driver accepts a ride request through the RAJLO platform, the driver is expected to make reasonable efforts to complete the trip professionally and safely.
2.2 Permitted Cancellations
Drivers may cancel a trip when reasonably necessary, including but not limited to:
Passenger no-show
Safety concerns
Mechanical failure
Medical emergencies
Dangerous weather or road conditions
Incorrect pickup information
Rider misconduct or threatening behavior
Network or technical failures
2.3 Excessive Cancellations
Frequent or repeated cancellations that negatively impact platform reliability may result in:
Warnings
Temporary account restrictions
Reduced trip priority
Temporary suspension
Permanent deactivation from the platform
RAJLO reserves the right to review cancellation activity on a case-by-case basis.

3. Late Arrival & Pickup Standards
3.1 Timeliness
Drivers are expected to proceed toward pickup locations promptly after accepting ride requests and to maintain professional service standards.
3.2 Communication with Riders
If delayed, drivers should communicate with riders through the RAJLO app whenever possible.
RAJLO is not responsible for delays caused by:
Traffic congestion
Road closures
GPS inaccuracies
Weather conditions
Rider location errors
Mobile network outages
Events outside the driver's control

4. Passenger No-Show Policy
4.1 Waiting Time
Drivers are encouraged to wait a reasonable amount of time at the pickup location before canceling a ride due to passenger absence.
4.2 No-Show Documentation
Drivers may be required to confirm arrival through GPS, timestamp data, or in-app tools before a no-show cancellation is processed.

5. Safety & Emergency Situations
5.1 Driver Safety Priority
Drivers should never continue a trip if they believe their personal safety, the passenger’s safety, or public safety is at risk.
5.2 Emergency Reporting
Drivers should immediately contact local emergency services in serious situations involving:
Accidents
Assault
Threats
Criminal activity
Medical emergencies
RAJLO may provide platform support but is not liable for emergency response outcomes.

6. Technical Issues
6.1 Platform Availability
RAJLO does not guarantee uninterrupted availability of the app, GPS systems, mobile connectivity, payment processing, or dispatch systems.
Temporary outages, delays, or technical errors may occur.
6.2 Driver Responsibility
Drivers are responsible for maintaining:
A functioning smartphone
Reliable internet or mobile data connection
Updated app software
Adequate battery power and device functionality

7. Compliance with Jamaican Laws
Drivers are solely responsible for complying with:
Jamaican Road Traffic laws
Transport Authority regulations
Licensing requirements
Insurance obligations
Vehicle fitness requirements
Tax obligations and business registration requirements
RAJLO is not responsible for any fines, penalties, impoundments, legal claims, or enforcement actions involving drivers.

8. Account Reviews & Enforcement
RAJLO reserves the right to investigate:
Excessive cancellations
Fraudulent activity
Unsafe driving behavior
Abuse of the platform
Violations of community guidelines
False reporting or manipulation of trips
RAJLO may suspend or permanently deactivate accounts at its sole discretion to protect platform integrity and user safety.

Cancellations
