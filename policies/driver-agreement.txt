RAJLO Driver Agreement
Effective Date: May 17, 2026
This Driver Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into between RAJLO (“RAJLO,” “Company,” “Platform,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) and the independent driver (“Driver,” “you,” or “your”) using the RAJLO platform within Jamaica.
By registering as a driver, accessing the RAJLO Driver App, accepting ride requests, or using the RAJLO platform in any manner, you agree to be legally bound by this Agreement.

1. Nature of the Platform
1.1 Technology Marketplace Platform
RAJLO is a technology platform that connects riders with independent third-party transportation providers.
RAJLO does not:
Provide transportation services directly
Employ drivers
Operate vehicles
Control driver schedules
Supervise routes or driving decisions
Guarantee ride requests or earnings
Drivers independently provide transportation services to riders.

2. Independent Contractor Relationship
2.1 Independent Contractor Status
Drivers using the RAJLO platform are independent contractors and are not:
Employees of RAJLO
Agents of RAJLO
Partners or joint venturers
Representatives of RAJLO
Nothing in this Agreement creates:
Employment rights
Salary entitlement
Pension rights
Health benefits
Vacation pay
Overtime compensation
Workers’ compensation coverage
2.2 Driver Control
Drivers maintain full control over:
Work hours
Availability
Acceptance of ride requests
Routes taken
Vehicle operation
Working locations within Jamaica
Drivers may choose when and whether to use the RAJLO platform.

3. Driver Eligibility Requirements
Drivers must:
Possess a valid Jamaican driver’s license
Maintain all legally required permits and authorizations
Comply with Jamaican Transport Authority regulations
Maintain legally required insurance coverage
Operate a roadworthy vehicle
Maintain accurate account information
Successfully complete any onboarding or verification requirements required by RAJLO
RAJLO reserves the right to approve, reject, suspend, or deactivate driver accounts at its sole discretion.

4. Driver Responsibilities
Drivers are solely responsible for:
Safe vehicle operation
Passenger safety
Vehicle maintenance and cleanliness
Fuel and operating expenses
Mobile devices and internet connectivity
Taxes and statutory obligations
Compliance with Jamaican laws and regulations
Professional conduct toward riders
Drivers agree to provide transportation services lawfully, safely, and professionally.

5. Vehicle Requirements
Drivers must ensure that vehicles used on the platform:
Are legally registered
Are roadworthy and safe
Meet Jamaican legal requirements
Maintain valid fitness and insurance documentation
Are clean and reasonably maintained
Meet any RAJLO platform standards communicated from time to time

6. RAJLO Services
RAJLO currently supports:
RAJLO Private Ride — on-demand private transportation
RAJLO Route — route-based transportation operating on Jamaican taxi routes and fare systems
RAJLO reserves the right to modify, suspend, add, or remove services at any time.

Drivers are eligible to earn fares generated from completed trips facilitated through the RAJLO platform.

7.3 No Earnings Guarantee
RAJLO does not guarantee:

Minimum earnings

Availability of trip requests

Market demand
Driver availability
Competition
Location
Traffic conditions
Rider behavior
Drivers assume all financial risks associated with operating independently.
