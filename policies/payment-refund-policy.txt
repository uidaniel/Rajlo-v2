RAJLO Payment & Refund Policy
Effective Date: May 17, 2026
Welcome to RAJLO — Jamaica’s First Cashless Transportation Platform.
This Payment & Refund Policy (“Policy”) governs all payments, fares, fees, refunds, cancellations, disputes, and financial transactions processed through the RAJLO platform, mobile applications, payment systems, and related services (collectively, the “Platform”).
By using the RAJLO Platform, riders and drivers agree to comply with this Policy.

1. Cashless Platform
1.1 Electronic Payments Only
RAJLO operates as a fully cashless transportation platform.
All trip fares, cancellation fees, service charges, and applicable platform fees are processed electronically through approved payment methods.
Cash payments outside the Platform are prohibited unless expressly authorized by RAJLO.
1.2 Authorized Payment Methods
Users must maintain a valid payment method linked to their account.
RAJLO may support:
Debit cards
Credit cards
Digital wallets
Bank-linked payment systems
Other approved electronic payment methods
RAJLO reserves the right to modify accepted payment methods at any time.

2. Fare Calculations
2.1 Fare Estimates
Displayed fares are estimates only and may change based on:
Distance traveled
Time elapsed
Traffic conditions
Route changes
Wait times
Pickup location changes
Mapping recalculations
Service demand
Technical adjustments
Applicable taxes or fees
Final fares are determined upon trip completion using RAJLO’s pricing systems.
2.2 RAJLO Route Pricing
RAJLO Route fares may utilize Jamaican route taxi structures, regional fare systems, or platform-adjusted pricing models.
2.3 RAJLO Private Ride Pricing
Private Ride pricing may vary based on:
Trip distance
Trip duration
Demand conditions
Pickup location
Driver availability
Operational factors
RAJLO reserves the right to modify pricing structures at any time.

3. Cancellation Fees
3.1 Rider Cancellation Fees
Cancellation fees may apply where:
A driver has accepted the request
The driver is traveling to the pickup location
The driver has arrived at the pickup location
The rider fails to appear
The rider causes unreasonable delays
Cancellation fees may vary depending on:
Distance traveled by the driver
Time elapsed
Service category
Demand conditions
Geographic area
3.2 Driver Cancellations
Drivers may cancel trips for legitimate operational or safety-related reasons.
RAJLO reserves the right to investigate cancellation activity and apply account restrictions where appropriate.

4. Automatic Charges
By using the Platform, users authorize RAJLO and its payment processors to:
Charge trip fares
Charge cancellation fees
Apply outstanding balances
Retry failed payments
Apply service fees
Process adjustments or corrections
Recover unpaid balances
Users agree that charges may occur automatically without additional authorization at the time of processing.

5. Failed Payments
5.1 Payment Authorization Failures
If a payment method fails, RAJLO may:
Retry payment processing
Restrict ride requests
Temporarily suspend accounts
Require updated payment information
Apply outstanding balances to future transactions
5.2 Outstanding Balances
Outstanding balances may prevent continued use of the Platform until resolved.
RAJLO reserves the right to pursue collection of unpaid amounts where permitted by law.

Fraud is suspected

Evidence does not support the claim
