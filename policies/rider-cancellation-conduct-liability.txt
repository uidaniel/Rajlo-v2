RAJLO Rider Cancellation, Conduct & Liability Policy
Effective Date: May 17, 2026
Welcome to RAJLO — Jamaica’s First Cashless Transportation Platform.
This Rider Cancellation, Conduct & Liability Policy (“Policy”) governs the use of the RAJLO platform, mobile applications, services, payment systems, and related technologies within Jamaica. By creating an account, requesting transportation services, or using the RAJLO platform, riders agree to comply with this Policy and all applicable Jamaican laws and regulations.

1. Platform Disclaimer & Independent Driver Status
1.1 Technology Platform Only
RAJLO is a technology marketplace platform that connects riders with independent third-party transportation providers.
RAJLO:
Does not own, lease, or operate vehicles
Does not employ drivers
Does not directly provide transportation services
Does not control driver behavior, routes, or driving decisions
Does not guarantee ride availability, timing, or uninterrupted service
All transportation services are independently provided by third-party drivers.
1.2 Independent Contractor Relationship
Drivers using the RAJLO platform are independent contractors and are not employees, agents, representatives, joint venturers, or partners of RAJLO.
Drivers are solely responsible for:
Vehicle operation
Driver conduct
Passenger interactions
Vehicle maintenance
Licensing and insurance
Compliance with Jamaican laws and Transport Authority regulations
Taxes, permits, and operational expenses
1.3 Assumption of Risk
By using the RAJLO platform, riders acknowledge that transportation services involve inherent risks, including but not limited to:
Traffic accidents
Vehicle malfunctions
Criminal acts
Driver negligence
Road hazards
Weather conditions
Delays
Technical failures
Riders voluntarily assume all risks associated with using third-party transportation services.

2. RAJLO Services
RAJLO currently offers the following transportation categories:
2.1 RAJLO Private Ride
A private on-demand transportation service connecting riders with independent drivers for direct trips.
2.2 RAJLO Route
A route-based transportation service operating on established Jamaican taxi routes and fare structures.
RAJLO reserves the right to add, remove, or modify services at any time.

3. Rider Cancellation Policy
3.1 Free Cancellation Window
Riders may cancel a trip within a limited grace period after booking without incurring a cancellation fee.
The grace period may vary depending on:
Driver proximity
Traffic conditions
Platform activity
Service type
3.2 Cancellation Fees
A cancellation fee may apply if:
A driver has accepted the trip
The driver is traveling toward the pickup location
The driver has arrived at the pickup location
The rider causes unreasonable delays
The rider fails to appear
The rider cannot be contacted
Cancellation fees may vary based on:
Distance traveled by the driver
Driver wait time
Service category
Time and demand conditions
Geographic location within Jamaica
3.3 No-Show Fees
If a rider fails to enter the vehicle within the designated waiting period, the trip may be canceled and a no-show fee may apply.
Estimated waiting standards may include:
RAJLO Route: Limited or no waiting period may apply
RAJLO Private Ride: Approx. 5–7 minutes depending on location and traffic conditions
RAJLO reserves the right to modify waiting periods at any time.
3.4 Repeat Cancellations
Excessive, abusive, or fraudulent cancellations may result in:
Temporary ride restrictions
Additional verification requirements
Reduced platform access
Temporary suspension
Permanent account deactivation
RAJLO may review cancellation patterns at its sole discretion.
