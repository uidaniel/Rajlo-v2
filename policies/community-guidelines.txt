RAJLO Community Guidelines
Effective Date: May 17, 2026
Welcome to RAJLO — Jamaica’s First Cashless Transportation Platform.
These Community Guidelines (“Guidelines”) establish the standards of conduct required for all riders, drivers, users, contractors, visitors, and participants using the RAJLO platform, mobile applications, websites, communication systems, and related services (collectively, the “Platform”).
These Guidelines are intended to help maintain a safe, respectful, lawful, and reliable environment throughout the RAJLO ecosystem.
By accessing or using the Platform, users agree to comply with these Guidelines, all applicable Jamaican laws, and all related RAJLO policies and agreements.

1. Respect & Professional Conduct
All users must behave respectfully, professionally, and lawfully at all times.
Users must not:
Harass, intimidate, or threaten others
Use abusive, offensive, or discriminatory language
Engage in bullying or hate speech
Engage in verbal or physical violence
Engage in sexual harassment or misconduct
Damage another person’s property
Create unsafe situations
Encourage illegal conduct
RAJLO maintains zero tolerance for violent, threatening, discriminatory, or abusive behavior.

2. Safety Standards
2.1 Driver Safety Responsibilities
Drivers must:
Operate vehicles safely and responsibly
Follow Jamaican traffic laws
Maintain valid licensing and insurance
Keep vehicles reasonably clean and roadworthy
Avoid distracted driving
Avoid impaired driving
Use lawful pickup and drop-off locations where reasonably possible
2.2 Rider Safety Responsibilities
Riders must:
Wear seatbelts where available
Avoid distracting drivers
Enter and exit vehicles safely
Follow reasonable safety instructions
Avoid unsafe or unlawful conduct during trips
2.3 Emergency Situations
In emergencies involving:
Accidents
Assaults
Threats
Criminal activity
Medical emergencies
users should immediately contact Jamaican emergency services.
RAJLO may provide limited support assistance but is not responsible for emergency response outcomes.

3. Illegal Activity Prohibited
Users may not use the Platform for:
Criminal activity
Human trafficking
Drug distribution or illegal drug activity
Transportation of illegal weapons or contraband
Fraudulent activity
Money laundering
Theft or robbery
Smuggling
Harassment or stalking
Any conduct violating Jamaican law
RAJLO reserves the right to cooperate with law enforcement where permitted or required by law.

4. Fraud & Platform Manipulation
The following activities are strictly prohibited:
Fake ride requests
Fake trip completion
GPS spoofing or fake location systems
Fare manipulation
Referral fraud
Payment fraud
Chargeback abuse
Identity fraud
Account sharing without authorization
Multiple fraudulent accounts
Artificial ride inflation
Manipulation of ratings or reviews
Use of bots or unauthorized automation
Attempts to bypass platform fees
RAJLO reserves the right to investigate suspicious activity and permanently remove users engaged in fraudulent conduct.

5. Driver Conduct Standards
Drivers must:
Treat riders respectfully
Maintain professional behavior
Avoid discrimination
Avoid harassment or intimidation
Avoid reckless driving
Avoid intoxication or impairment
Maintain vehicle cleanliness and safety
Comply with Jamaican transport regulations
Drivers may not:
Solicit cash payments outside the Platform
Carry illegal weapons or contraband
Use another person’s account
Permit unauthorized drivers to use their account
Refuse service unlawfully
Engage in threatening or criminal conduct

6. Rider Conduct Standards
Riders must:
Treat drivers respectfully
Provide accurate pickup information
Maintain lawful conduct during trips
Avoid damaging vehicles
Maintain professional behavior
Riders may not:
Threaten or harass drivers
Damage vehicles
Smoke in vehicles where prohibited
Consume illegal drugs
Carry illegal weapons or contraband
Engage in fraudulent activity
Create unsafe conditions during trips
Riders may be financially responsible for:
Vehicle damage
Excessive cleaning fees
Repair costs
Safety-related incidents caused by rider misconduct

7. Discrimination & Harassment
RAJLO prohibits discrimination or harassment based on characteristics protected under applicable law.
Users may not engage in:
Racist conduct
Threatening behavior
Sexual harassment
Hate speech
Religious harassment
Disability discrimination
Any abusive or discriminatory conduct
RAJLO reserves the right to suspend or permanently ban accounts involved in discriminatory or harassing behavior.

8. Alcohol, Drugs & Weapons
Users may not:
Operate vehicles while impaired
Consume illegal drugs during trips
Carry illegal weapons or contraband
Engage in unlawful substance-related conduct
Drivers must never operate vehicles while impaired by:
Alcohol
Illegal drugs
Controlled substances affecting driving ability
RAJLO maintains zero tolerance for impaired driving.

9. Account Security & Identity Verification
Users are responsible for:
Protecting account credentials
Protecting OTP verification codes
Maintaining accurate account information
Preventing unauthorized account access
Users may not:
Impersonate others
Create fake identities
Use stolen payment methods
Share accounts without authorization
RAJLO reserves the right to require identity verification at any time.

10. Vehicle Standards
Drivers must maintain vehicles that are:
Roadworthy
Legally registered
Reasonably clean
Safe for transportation services
Properly insured where required by law
RAJLO reserves the right to remove vehicles from the Platform where safety or compliance concerns arise.

11. Privacy & Recording
Users must respect the privacy of others.
Users may not:
Share personal information without authorization
Publicly disclose private trip information
Misuse screenshots, recordings, or user data
Harass users outside the Platform
RAJLO reserves the right to investigate privacy-related complaints.

12. Ratings, Reviews & Reporting
Users are expected to provide honest and accurate ratings, reports, and complaints.
False reports, fraudulent complaints, or abuse of safety reporting systems may result in:
Account restrictions
Suspension
Permanent deactivation
RAJLO reserves the right to investigate all reports at its sole discretion.

13. Technical Abuse Prohibited
Users may not:
Reverse engineer the Platform
Attempt unauthorized access to systems
Interfere with Platform operations
Introduce malware or harmful code
Scrape Platform data
Exploit software vulnerabilities
Circumvent Platform protections
RAJLO reserves all rights relating to Platform systems, software, and infrastructure.

14. Enforcement & Investigations
RAJLO reserves the right to:
Investigate violations
Monitor Platform activity
Review trip activity
Review payment activity
Suspend or terminate accounts
Remove content or access
Cooperate with law enforcement
Take legal action where appropriate
RAJLO may take enforcement action without prior notice where necessary to protect:
User safety
Platform integrity
Operational systems
Legal compliance

15. Account Suspension & Permanent Bans
Violations of these Guidelines may result in:
Warnings
Temporary restrictions
Account suspension
Permanent deactivation
Payment restrictions
Legal action
RAJLO reserves the right to determine appropriate enforcement measures at its sole discretion.

16. No Guarantee of Safety
While RAJLO strives to promote safety and responsible conduct, RAJLO cannot guarantee:
User behavior
Driver behavior
Passenger behavior
Crime prevention
Vehicle safety
Background check accuracy
Emergency response outcomes
Platform security
Users voluntarily assume all risks associated with using the Platform.

RAJLO, its owners, affiliates, officers, directors, employees, contractors, licensors, and service providers shall not be liable for:
User misconduct
Criminal acts
Assaults
Theft
Harassment
Vehicle accidents
Property damage
Personal injury
Emotional distress
Technical failures
Third-party disputes
Off-platform conduct
Law enforcement actions
Users use the Platform entirely at their own risk.

18. Policy Updates
RAJLO reserves the right to modify or update these Community Guidelines at any time without prior notice.
Continued use of the Platform following updates constitutes acceptance of the revised Guidelines.

19. Governing Law
These Guidelines shall be governed by and interpreted in accordance with the laws of Jamaica.
Any disputes relating to these Guidelines shall be subject to the jurisdiction of the courts of Jamaica.

20. User Acknowledgment
By using the RAJLO Platform, users acknowledge and agree that they:
Have read and understood these Community Guidelines
Agree to comply with all Platform rules and Jamaican laws
Accept all risks associated with third-party transportation services
Understand that violations may result in account suspension or permanent deactivation
Release RAJLO from liability to the fullest extent permitted under Jamaican law
