RAJLO Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)
Effective Date: May 17, 2026
Welcome to RAJLO — Jamaica’s First Cashless Transportation Platform.
This Acceptable Use Policy (“AUP”) governs the permitted and prohibited use of the RAJLO platform, mobile applications, websites, APIs, servers, software systems, payment systems, communication systems, and related services (collectively, the “Platform”).
This AUP is designed to protect the security, reliability, integrity, and lawful operation of the Platform.
By accessing or using the Platform, users agree to comply with this AUP, all related RAJLO policies, and applicable Jamaican laws.

1. Authorized Use Only
Users may use the Platform only for lawful, authorized, and intended transportation-related purposes.
Users may not use the Platform in ways that:
Violate Jamaican law
Threaten Platform security
Disrupt Platform operations
Interfere with other users
Abuse Platform systems
Facilitate fraud or criminal activity
RAJLO reserves the right to determine whether activity violates this AUP.

2. Fraudulent Activity Prohibited
The following activities are strictly prohibited:
Fake ride requests
Fake trip generation
Fake driver activity
Fake rider accounts
Fraudulent referrals
Promotional abuse
Chargeback fraud
Payment fraud
Stolen payment method usage
Fare manipulation
Artificial surge manipulation
Identity fraud
Impersonation
Fake reviews or ratings
Coordinated fraud schemes
RAJLO reserves the right to investigate suspected fraud and cooperate with law enforcement where appropriate.

3. GPS Spoofing & Location Manipulation
Users may not:
Use fake GPS applications
Manipulate device location data
Falsify trip locations
Use VPNs or tools intended to evade fraud detection systems
Interfere with mapping or tracking systems
Attempt to bypass geolocation restrictions
RAJLO may monitor for suspicious location behavior and permanently deactivate accounts engaged in GPS manipulation.

4. Unauthorized Automation & Bots
Users may not:
Use bots or automated scripts
Automate ride requests
Automate driver acceptance systems
Use unauthorized macros or automation tools
Scrape Platform content or data
Use crawler systems or indexing bots without authorization
Conduct automated account creation
Abuse APIs or backend systems
Unauthorized automation is strictly prohibited.

5. Reverse Engineering & System Interference
Users may not:
Reverse engineer Platform systems
Decompile software
Copy Platform code or systems
Attempt unauthorized access to servers
Circumvent Platform protections
Interfere with Platform infrastructure
Introduce malware, spyware, or harmful code
Probe Platform vulnerabilities
Conduct denial-of-service attacks
Attempt unauthorized data extraction
RAJLO reserves all rights relating to its software, infrastructure, algorithms, and intellectual property.

6. Account Abuse Prohibited
Users may not:
Share accounts without authorization
Sell or transfer accounts
Operate multiple fraudulent accounts
Use another person’s identity
Access accounts without authorization
Use stolen credentials
Circumvent account suspensions
Create accounts for fraudulent purposes
RAJLO may suspend or permanently deactivate accounts engaged in abusive activity.

7. Payment System Abuse
Users may not:
Abuse refund systems
Engage in chargeback fraud
Manipulate payment systems
Attempt unauthorized transactions
Use stolen payment methods
Circumvent Platform fees
Exploit pricing errors knowingly
RAJLO reserves the right to recover losses arising from fraudulent or abusive payment activity.

8. Security Monitoring
RAJLO reserves the right to monitor Platform activity for:
Fraud prevention
GPS spoofing detection
Unauthorized access attempts
Automation detection
Security threats
Payment abuse
Suspicious account behavior
Infrastructure protection
Users acknowledge and consent to reasonable monitoring and security enforcement activities.

9. Intellectual Property Protection

Reproduce Platform interfaces
Misuse RAJLO trademarks
Republish Platform content without authorization
Create unauthorized derivative systems
Commercially exploit Platform systems
All Platform intellectual property remains the exclusive property of RAJLO.

10. API & Infrastructure Restrictions
Unless expressly authorized in writing, users may not:
Access internal APIs
Interfere with backend systems
Conduct penetration testing
Overload servers intentionally
Attempt infrastructure exploitation
Circumvent technical limitations
Access administrative systems
RAJLO reserves the right to block IP addresses, devices, or networks involved in suspicious activity.

11. Data Protection & Privacy
Users may not:
Collect personal information without authorization
Harvest Platform user data
Share confidential user information
Sell Platform data
Misuse trip or location information
Conduct unauthorized surveillance
RAJLO reserves the right to investigate privacy violations.

12. Criminal Activity Prohibited
The Platform may not be used for:
Human trafficking
Drug distribution
Transportation of illegal contraband
Money laundering
Organized fraud schemes
Cybercrime
Harassment or stalking
Violent criminal activity
